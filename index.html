Life Story

"Love your neighbor as yourself."

NIKKO AGURA SUMBISE


The “Artisan”, sharp in handling paint brushes, color pencils and drafting pens. He has been dreaming of being part of Pixar ( Computer Animation Company). With this high ambition, he trained himself in digital art nowadays. His talent is as always align with his personality; creative, imaginative, bold, serene, and warm. Nikko sees his friends like family. He treasured moments with them. He never fails us as a friend.

MITCHEL MANTILLA


Mitchel Mantilla was once a Student-athlete during elementary and high school years. With sports experience, she have been to many places to compete and giving her time for leisure at the same time. By that, traveling has now become one of her goal.

NEILJAY DAHAM


Neiljay Daham has a very deceiving appearance for being “maldito” but those are the things that make him beautiful in the eyes of people who know him. The way he look is the total opposite of being him. He may look “maldito” but he is a person full of concern to his friends and his family. He has a heart of Gold and a very supportive friend to his friends.

ERICA BUSANO


Erica Busano is from Maramag Bukidnon. A woman with a Golden Voice. A woman who sang with her heart, and uses a song as tool to express her feeeligs and herself. A tool which would bring the Barkada’s mouth in unison. A woman that would soothes your aching heart as her voice reaches your ear. A very supportive friend who would always be there to help you when your down, and would give you a little enlightenment from her own experience in life.

JOHN MARK LADERA


John Mark Ladera, youngest in the family, lives from Lantapan, Bukidnon. A loving man to all his friends and family. He shares the sweetest smile to all the people he knew, and he just met. A man living with his principles is now living independently. A man who would willingly extend his hand to all his friends in need.

GILVERA RAVELLO


Gilvera Ravello is from Negros Occidental. She’s a loving and caring girl. She makes mistakes, she cry and get hurt. But that's her way of getting more stronger and becomes a better person for her friends, family and for those people who truly believes and trust her. She's a trustworthy woman with a great personality.

JERALD LUZONG GUADAYO


Jerald Luzong Guadayo is from Barangay Maingaran, Masbate City, Masbate. A kind hearted person who perfectly socialize with people through his jokes. A man of Happiness and Love. The man who can easily crack jokes and can immediately put you into burst of laughters.

MARYJOY BERDARA


Maryjoy Berdera is an easy going person from Tandag City, Surigao del Sur.. Maryjoy’s hyperactive personality has drawn everyone to her company. She has he sense of humor and a very approachable person. Her physical appearance may deceive people who don’t know her for being a silent type of person but the truth is she is a very talkative person who loves to go places to places.

DAVE LENNEN ARDIENTE


Dave Lennen Ardiente, in his musically artistic hands, struck his guitar flawlessly. He is the group's Big Brother. He played his guitar passionately, to express the emotion he is feeling. Every single struck of the strings brings the vibration of happiness and positivity to the group. In his childlike way of bringing his self reflects from his birthplace, Zamboanga del Sur.

JOHNLOYD PIODO


Johnloyd “bata” Piodo, the youngest in the group, lives in Bagumbayan, Dinagat Islands. He was also a player during her high school years. He joined the Sepak Takraw team in their school and was appointed as the Team Leader. His concern does not limit to one friend, he cares for everybody. Moreover, his positive mindset always lifts negativity into positive ones. For him, everything is possible.

MARY GRACE PALAD


Mary Grace Clava Palad from Sitio Anislagon Monte Alegre Matalom Leyte. A very understanding and God fearing woman. She loves to share what she have to other people even with her little things. A simple woman with a contiguous smile.
