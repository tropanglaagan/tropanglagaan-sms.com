    A PERFECT STRANGERS

    A PERFECT STRANGERS at start. We never knew each other during our High School days though we are from the same Alma Mater ( Sisters of Mary School). Quiet strange? That is because we have different campuses ( Girlstown and Boystown). It is Time and Space that somehow collide us. We met after our graduation, that was on November 10th 2019 when we first had our meeting. It was one of our friend's birthday invitation. And that was then we started sharing the same vibes; Travel, Humors, Goals and Dreams!
